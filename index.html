About NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh Foundation is a government registered NGO (80G & 12A) striving to empower underprivileged sections, spread welfare, and build community strength.

Feed India Drive

Distributing thousands of healthy, cooked meals daily to underprivileged families and conducting animal-feeding drives in municipal shelter areas.

Primary Education

Providing basic schooling, books, writing materials, and educational camps to young children living in slums and remote villages.

Hygiene & Health

Organizing sanitary pad distribution camps for rural women and conducting awareness webinars regarding primary reproductive health.

Animal Welfare

Setting up medical camps for injured stray animals, providing treatment, shelter support, and winter warmth initiatives.

Tech Internship FAQs

The standard internship duration is 2-3 months, depending on the track and milestone completions. It can be extended based on mutual consent and performance.

NayePankh Foundation offers both voluntary (unpaid) opportunities to contribute to social causes and selective project-based stipends for exceptional tech deliverables. Details of stipends are mentioned in the final selection letter.
